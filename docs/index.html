Privacy Policy for Doodl

Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Doodl is a simple drawing app. We take your privacy seriously — which is easy, because we don't collect any data.

Data Collection

Doodl does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account required — you don't need to sign up or log in
  • No analytics — we don't track how you use the app
  • No advertising — there are no ads and no ad tracking
  • No network requests — the app works completely offline
  • No cookies or identifiers — we don't use device IDs or tracking pixels

Data Storage

All drawings are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded to any server. If you delete the app, your drawings are permanently deleted.

Children's Privacy

Doodl does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Doodl does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
